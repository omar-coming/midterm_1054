Argument against cats

Cats are about as annoying as an animal can get, they have sharp claws, sadnpaper tongues and they shit in your house on a good day. Not to mention the hairballs, ruined furniture, scratches on your arms and sleepless nights that are sure to enthrall you with joyous emotions.

Argument for cats

We put up with them because: Below the entitlement, maliciousness, and monotony of their behavior; There is a friend that no human could ever hope to match. A buddy to conquer the world with. Also, nothing says adorable like cats. Their baggy jumpsuit-esque skin that's floppily overlaid on their fragile yet fierce skeletal structure. They're pretty much overgrown squirrels.
